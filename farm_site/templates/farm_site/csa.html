CSA

We are now accepting new and returning members for our 2017 CSA season. This is our eleventh year farming and offering our well-loved CSA. All our produce is grown by us, here on our farm. We have pickup sites throughout Seattle, as well on-farm pickup. Our season is 26 weeks from June to November.

Why join our CSA?

Happy CSA members: Each year, about 75% of our members re-join. Our year-end member survey consistently shows very high levels of satisfaction with our produce selection, quality, and value for price.

Longer CSA season: We’ve invested in storage infrastructure on our farm that enables us to provide diverse vegetable offerings all the way through November. For 2017 our CSA season is 26 weeks long, and we’re always trying new varieties and new storage methods to extend the season even further.

What's in a box?

We offer two box sizes. The standard box is suitable for a household of two people who cook at home three to four nights a week. The standard box includes 7-10 items (e.g. 1 head lettuce, 1 bunch kale, 1# carrots) each week. The large box option typically has one or two additional items and/or larger quantities of staple items like salad greens, potatoes, squash, broccoli and green beans.

Price

Standard box: $570

Large box: $715

This works out to just under $22 per box for the standard size and $27.50 for the large size. This works out to about a 15% disocunt compared to buying these items at a grocery store or farmers market.

When you sign up, you will have the option to mail us a check or choose one of various online options (like through your bank's online bill pay service).

Eggs

We partner with Goose and Gander Farm to offer a half-dozen or dozen eggs as a 20-week-add-on.
