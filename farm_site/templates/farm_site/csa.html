Community-Supported Agriculture

2017 is our 11th season offering our popular CSA program. We grow over 50 vegetables and herbs in our mild climate and world-class soil. CSA subscribers receive a box of freshly harvested vegetables once a week during the growing season.

Subscription Details

Season: 26 weeks (June to November)

Price: $500 for standard box, $700 for large box

Option to add on a dozen or half-dozen eggs from Goose & Gander farm

Pick up from 15 locations in the greater Seattle area

Why join our CSA?

Happy CSA members

Each year, about 75% of our members re-join. Our year-end member survey consistently shows very high levels of satisfaction with our produce selection, quality, and value for price.

Longer CSA season

We’ve invested in storage infrastructure on our farm that enables us to provide diverse vegetable offerings all the way through November. For 2017 our CSA season is 26 weeks long, and we’re always trying new varieties and new storage methods to extend the season even further.

We grow everything

We love to grow and eat a whole lot of different vegetables. We work hard to provide our CSA members with a wide variety of produce throughout the growing season.

What's in a box?

We offer two box sizes. The standard box is suitable for a household of two people who cook at home three to four nights a week. The large box option typically has one or two additional items and/or larger quantities of staple items like salad greens, potatoes, squash, broccoli and green beans.

Price

Our pricing works out to just under $22 per box for the standard size and $27.50 for the large size. This works out to about a 15% disocunt compared to buying these items at a grocery store or farmers market.

When you sign up, you will have the option to mail us a check or choose one of various online options (like through your bank's online bill pay service).
